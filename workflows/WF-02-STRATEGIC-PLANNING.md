# WF-02: STRATEGIC IMPROVEMENT PLANNING

## Workflow Purpose
Monthly planning session that converts 4 weeks of request intelligence into an actionable improvement roadmap.

**Strategic Goal:** Shift team from 60% reactive to 40% reactive work through:
- Automation of high-volume repeatable work
- Enterprise datasets that eliminate recurring requests
- Self-service tools that empower business users
- Standardization that improves efficiency and quality

## Workflow Execution

### STEP 1: Gather Monthly Inputs (1st of month, 30 min)
**Collect:**
- [ ] 4 weekly digests from WF-01
- [ ] Current roadmap status
- [ ] Team capacity assessment
- [ ] Business priority updates

### STEP 2: Synthesize Insights (45 min)
**Skill:** SK-AGGREGATE-MONTHLY-INSIGHTS
- Aggregate 4 weeks of patterns
- Identify persistent vs. one-off opportunities
- Track team evolution metrics

### STEP 3: Prioritize Roadmap (45 min)
**Skill:** SK-PRIORITIZE-IMPROVEMENT-ROADMAP
- Balance quick wins vs. strategic initiatives
- Allocate team capacity
- Set monthly targets

### STEP 4: Generate Planning Document (30 min)
**Output:** Monthly roadmap markdown file
**Save to:** Notion + GitHub

**Total Time:** 2.5 hours monthly

## Success Metrics
- 1-2 automations implemented per month
- 1 enterprise initiative per quarter
- Reactive work % declining month-over-month
- Team capacity freed for strategic work
